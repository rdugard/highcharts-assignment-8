ADHD in Children

Attention Deficit Hiperactive Disorder (ADHD), also known as Attention Deficit Disorder (ADD) is a disorder that is characterized by inattentiveness, hyperactivity and impulsiveness. The symptoms usually appear before a child is seven years old and is diagnosed by a doctor. There is no difinitive test for ADHD, but studies show that the percentage of children diagnosed with ADHD has grown recently.
